Performance Enhancement of PMMA/GOx Hybrid Rocket Engine Using Swirling Injection.

Posted on 2022-05-01 Edited on 2025-09-20

Introduction

Hybrid rocket is safer than liquid rocket, and its specific impulse is higher than solid rocket. In this study, we focus on developing a PMMA/GOx hybrid rocket engine with a swirling injector and design the injector for increasing the combustion efficiency. Proved by the experiments and analyses, the combustion characteristics and engine performance
can be enhanced by swirl-injection design.

Purposes

  1. Design a hybrid rocket engine (HRE).
  2. Investigate the combustion characteristics of PMMA.
  3. Investigate the relation between engine performance and geometry swirling number (SNg).

Conclusions

  1. The performance of SNg 7 hybrid rocket engine is listed as C* 1875 Cf 0 80 F 5 5 kgf, Isp 170 5 s, ሶሶr 2 54 mm/s Compared to an axial injection engine, the performance of SNg 7 engine has significant enhancement as listed Cf increases 77 thrust increases 149 Isp increases 203 and engine efficiency increases from 26 to 80.
  2. Compared to other swirl injection engine, our engine has higher regression rate which is three times higher than the reference one, under properly designed SNg With the advantage of our
    swirl injection design, we can have higher fuel mass flow rate and hence larger thrust and higher engine efficiency when the fuel length is the same.
